# MPP

The MPP is the Promo Landing Page contains the description of the promotion, and on how to join for the customers.

## Creating The MPP

Navigate to the MPP directory:

<Admonition type="info" title="Sitecore Path">
  Content > Vanilla.Mobile > State/Brand > MobilePortal-v1.0 > Promotions > Casino
</Admonition>

Duplicate the `mpp-template-manila` and then rename it to your `url`. Also rename the child component with your `url`.

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dlfu36fiw/image/upload/v1709887475/builds/mpp/x67k1mpo40skfgod9scc.png)

## Parent MPP

Click on "Edit rule", it opens the "Create Rule" box. Click on the highlighted text and it will open another box. Browse to your MPP's child and select it, then press OK. And Save.

Below is the step by step illustration of what you'll see when navigating.

### DetailedDescription

Click the "Edit HTML" to open the HTML Editor.

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dlfu36fiw/image/upload/v1709887473/builds/mpp/sahullwz0hmehtunpfrs.png)

First `<p>` tag, replace the text with the `Promotion Description`.

```html
<p><a class="btn btn-primary" data-offer-id="PAT ID" data-offer-message-error="Technical Error"
    data-offer-message-expired="Expired" data-offer-message-invalid="Invalid"
    data-offer-message-not-offered="Not Offered" data-offer-message-offered="Opt In"
    data-offer-message-opted-in="Opted In" data-offer-message-opted-out="Opted Out" data-offer-type="promos">Opt In</a> <a class="btn btn-primary" href="!!M2.Promo/offers/grouped?filter=cp">See My Progress</a></p>
```

Second `<p>` tag, [CTA](https://coralracing-my.sharepoint.com/\:x:/g/personal/krisha_manalo_entaingroup_com/EfvRGdc4sN5MiwcmKjBu55wBGvjTEIDYp-1XEU-iLJkGXA) will depend on the ticket.

For tickets that require an Opt-In CTA here is a sample of the updated CTA format to use.

  Check [CTA Docs Page](https://coralracing-my.sharepoint.com/\:x:/g/personal/krisha_manalo_entaingroup_com/EfvRGdc4sN5MiwcmKjBu55wBGvjTEIDYp-1XEU-iLJkGXA) for an exhaustive list of options.
