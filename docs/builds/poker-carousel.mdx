# Poker Carousel

The Poker Carousel is the main attraction of the Poker Page. It shows all the latest promos, Public and Targetted, to the customers.  

<img src={CaroPreview} width="100%" alt="Sample Carousel" /> 
*Highlighted in red box is the Carousel*

In this section, we will provide the necessary steps in building the Poker Carousel.

## Building The Carousel

To create your Carousel, navigate to it's directory:

:::info[SiteCore Path]
sitecore > content > Vanilla.Mobile > state/brand > MobilePoker-v1.0 > PokerTeasers
:::

### Step 1: Duplicate Carousel Object

Duplicate one of the Carousel object, preferably the poker-manila-template.

<img src={CaroDupRen} width="50%" alt="Duplicating a Carousel" /> 
*Right-click your desired carousel object, choose duplicate, then rename with your Ticket Name*

### Step 2: Fill in Teaser & Background Field

#### Required Fields:
|Field | Value|
|---|---|
|Title| CAROUSEL H1 (from the Copy)|
|Subtitle| CAROUSEL H2 (from the Copy)|
|Image|	Media URL: *Carousel PNG-605x328|
|BackgroundImage| Media URL: Poker Carousel-1210x328|

:::warning[Reminder]
Check your Carousel Assets. *If **Poker Carousel baked -1210x328_** is available, then replaced the Asset Carousel PNG-605x328 with a **Blank PNG** with size 605x328.
:::
