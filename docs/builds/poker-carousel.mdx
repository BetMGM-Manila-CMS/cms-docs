# Poker Carousel

The Poker Carousel is the main attraction of the Poker Page. It shows all the latest promos, Public and Targetted, to the customers.

![](/img/builds/poker-carousel/carouselpreview.png)

*Highlighted in red box is the Carousel*

In this section, we will provide the necessary steps in building the Poker Carousel.

## Building The Carousel

To create your Carousel, navigate to it's directory:

<Admonition type="note" title="Sitecore Path">
  sitecore > content > Vanilla.Mobile > state/brand > MobilePoker-v1.0 > PokerTeasers
</Admonition>

### Step 1: Duplicate Carousel Object

Duplicate one of the Carousel object, preferably the poker-manila-template.

![](/img/builds/poker-carousel/carouselduplicaterename.png)

*Right-click your desired carousel object, choose duplicate, then rename with your Ticket Name*

### Step 2: Fill in Links

If your build have an MPP or Tourney Page, the Links will be smartlink of your page. Otherwise, check [CTA Guide](cta.mdx) for all the List of CTAs.

![](/img/builds/poker-carousel/carositecore-2.png)

### Step 3: Filter

Don't forget to put filter for new builds

```html
QueryString.Get('qa') = 'yourname'
```

### Check your Carousel

To check your carousel, go to your ticket's state website, then put `?qa=yourname` like see below:

```html
https://poker.nj.betmgm.com/en/poker?qa=yourname
```

<Admonition type="tip" title="PRO TIP">
  Adding this to the URL will lessen your waiting time to check if your Carousel if already Up.

  ```javascript
  &_nocache=1
  ```
</Admonition>
